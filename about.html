About Me

Hello! I'm a Chennaiite by birth and heart, Telugu by ancestry and lived a few years in Mumbai, Pune and Michigan, USA. I'm a 1st year undergraduate at IIIT-Hyderabad who loves deep theoretical or philosophical dicussions. Having been a math enthusiast for as long as I can remember, exposure to the abstract world of real analysis and linear algebra was just what I needed to dive into the exciting, mystical world of quantum mechanics and computing. Ensuring communication holds strong in the face of a quantum adversary spoke to me and the abstract math cryptography offered was invigorating.

My research interests include:

  • Quantum algorithm development
  • Quantum query complexity and complexity classes
  • Post-quantum cryptography

When I'm not pouring over quantum supremacy, you can find me playing badminton or grinding blitz games on chess.com (send me a challenge @tarMath18!).
